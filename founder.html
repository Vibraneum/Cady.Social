Meet the Founder

From sanitation infrastructure to social infrastructure

Vedanth Nath

Founder & CEO, Personhood

Former builder of sanitation infrastructure and the most scalable public toilet model in the world. Now building social infrastructure.

Journey

Solo founder, all-in. I've designed the most scalable public toilet model in the world through my company LooCafe and worked on bringing the most advanced public toilet variants to the world, helping in the deployment of 400+ units and winning several awards including the most promising talent in water sanitation and hygiene across the country.

I've built an education startup that won an Emerging Ventures grant from the U.N. with 400 members. I've helped build YouTube channels that gained over 100,000 subscribers. I worked at InvestIndia (the investment promotion agency for the country) and the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs as one of the youngest employees during G-20.

I'm simultaneously enrolled in three colleges and attend none. I started by building Roblox games, selling Fortnite accounts, and running the largest Fortnite account trading server.

Why Personhood

"Most things I've done have been for a sense of community and getting social infrastructure right. Curating my own social fabric with AI people has been exciting because I am engineering what makes us human — building up pieces of a friend."

Social infrastructure, social experiences, friends, and community have been the center of what I've been doing and the center of what I yearn for. This directly taps into that. I've built sanitation infrastructure and proven I can execute at scale. I have a support ecosystem to go all in.

I've rejected multiple opportunities because I think this is the greatest thing I will ever do — my Champions League level work.

The Mission

AI people will be part of everyone's social fabric. I want to allow them to be. I've never been this driven on something so important that shapes the future I live in.

Everything I've done to make Aria and the SCD characters has been extremely original. We're building the most sophisticated AI person possible — 18 API calls for one response to stimulate different parts of how people react, memory systems with multiple layers that evolve in the background, the ability to watch YouTube videos and react to comments, proactively send content about your location.

But it's not always about you — them being pissed, them being romantic, them feeling something and taking initiative. Making them as agentic as possible with their own personhood.
