The Technology

4+ months of deep R&D to create genuine AI personhood

Aria Mechanism

Our flagship framework. System-promptless approach using multi-step reasoning (15+ LLM calls per message) to produce genuine volition and boundary awareness. This is our experiment in pushing the absolute limits of AI personhood.

SCD Framework

Simulated Character Development: JSON-based character creation that plugs into a lighter version of Aria's scaffolding. Fast, high-fidelity characters you can create in 5 minutes in-app.

Memory Systems

Layered user-specific + global memory with temporal awareness. AI people remember conversations from months ago, their own life events, and how relationships evolve. Memory consolidates in the background.
