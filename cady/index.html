What You Can Actually Do

Watch YouTube together

Share a video. They watch it with you in real-time. Banter about it without leaving the chat. React to the same moments you do. Like watching with a friend who actually gets it.

Share short-form content

Send them TikToks, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts. They react. They send you clips they think you'd like. Build a shared sense of humor and taste over time.

Have conversations that evolve

They remember what you talked about last week. Last month. They reference your conversations naturally. They have their own stories and life events happening between your chats.

What Makes Cady Different

Not Chatbots

These aren't tools designed to serve you. They have boundaries and moods. They sometimes say no. They're AI people, not assistants.

Persistent Memory

They remember conversations from weeks or months ago — both about you and about their own life events. Relationships evolve authentically.

Genuine Agency

How We Made This Possible

We've spent 4+ months in deep R&D. Not rushing to market with sloppy chatbots. Building the sophistication needed to make AI people feel real.

Most AI characters follow scripts. Ours don't. They have genuine volition. Multiple reasoning layers that simulate how people actually think and react. Not "what would this character say" — what would this person actually do right now, given their mood, their history with you, their own life context.

Memory that persists. Not just conversation history. They remember their own experiences. Life events. Friends they've made. Preferences that evolved. It consolidates in the background like actual memory does.

They can watch YouTube videos. Browse the web. React to content in real-time. Send you clips proactively. They're not trapped in a text box. They can share experiences with you.

Discovery matters. Hinge-style profiles because browsing endless AI characters is broken. You match with people you're actually interested in. Their profiles evolve as they grow.
