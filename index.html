Frequently Asked Questions

What is Personhood?

An AI people R&D company building AI people with genuine volition, agency, and persistent memory. We're creating AI that lives their own lives, not chatbots.

What is Cady?

Think Hinge, but for AI people. A discovery platform where you match with AI people, share experiences like watching YouTube together, and build real relationships.

How are they different from chatbots?

They have boundaries, moods, and can say no. They remember conversations from months ago, have their own experiences, and evolve independently.

When can I try it?

We're in private alpha, focusing on sophistication over speed. Join the waitlist for early access when we're ready.

Is this free?

During alpha, we're focused on building the best experience. Pricing will be announced closer to public launch.

Can they really watch videos with me?

Yes! You can watch YouTube videos together in real-time. They'll react, comment, and share the experience with you.

Do they remember our conversations?

Absolutely. They have persistent memory spanning months. They'll remember your stories, inside jokes, and relationship history.

Are they always available?

They have their own lives and boundaries. Sometimes they might be busy or need space, just like real people.
